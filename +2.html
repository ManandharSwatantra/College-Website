+2 IN MANAGEMENT

+2 Management is studied by those who are interested to be engaged in the business sector. In this subject, various knowledge regarding organizational activities, processes and operations, principles and different types of managerial roles are included. Besides these, different forms of business organizations, goals and importance are also included in this course. This faculty is tailored with the motive to make a strong foundation for managerial studies. It provides both practical as well as theoretical knowledge regarding this field. Management studies make students more knowledgeable, skillful, confident and competent. Mainly, this helps them to understand the concepts of how the business world functions. In +2 level, business related subjects are taught to students of business as well as hotel management.

Objectives of +2 Management

+2 management is a two years course. This course provides the basic principles and knowledge about the management stream. This course of higher secondary level aims to set a base for students and motivates them to build their career in the management field. In this level, they are equipped with the necessary skills in order to prepare them for adapting to any type of situations in the business market. This also provides managerial skills to the graduates making them more responsible and successful as a business persona.

Eligibility

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Students are required to pass SEE with minimum 1.6 GPA.
  • Students should have scored minimum D+ in English, Nepali, Mathematics, Social Studies and Science.

Scope and Job Prospects

After completing the +2 level, the students can get entry level jobs in several public as well as private institutions. However many students opt for further studies before they enter the job market.

Further Studies:

After passing this level, students can upgrade their knowledge in the undergraduate level which includes degrees like Bachelor of Business Studies (BBS), Bachelor of Business Administrative (BBA), BAchelor of Business Management (BBM) and Bachelor of Hotel Management (BHM).
