ABOUT US

Introduction

Ashirwad College (AC) is one of the renowned Management Colleges in Nepal which was established in 1999 A.D. (2056 BS) with a view to provide excellent quality in the Nepalese soil. We offer education in a moderate fee structure. The pioneer of this college is Mr. Dhruba Narayan Manandhar, an enthusiastic educational entrepreneur and a staunch humanist. AC holds the view that the development of any topography and civilization on this blue planet is possible only through globally competent quality education which can nurture time-driven quality education and humanistic values and the philanthropic worldview.

It is the first Hotel Management College that introduced the Hotel Management subject first time in the history of +2 Level in Nepal. The college has got its own building with a sound physical infrastructure 5 ropanies of compound which is required for a globally competent educational institution. This college has got a uniquely peaceful and student-friendly ambiance even in the midst of a Metropolis where we believe all flowers can blossom equally and interruptedly.
