Bachelor of Business Management (BBM)

BBM which stands for Bachelor of Business Management is actually a new program introduced by Tribhuvan University, Faculty of Management. BBM like that of BBA is a four year program comprising 8 semesters divided over 120 credit hours. This course is designed in such a way that this can be cornerstone for aspiring business leaders and qualified managers of future. The Bachelor of Business Management graduates are equipped with effective knowledge, abilities, self-confidence and skills to contribute in successful and efficient information management and transform themselves so as to fit in this highly changing, very competitive and volatile world.

Objectives of BBM

  • Skill development of students in various aspects like creative thinking, decision making, leadership, communication and increase understanding of operation and change.
  • Develop studentsâ€™ entrepreneurial and innovative capabilities and successful management of business operation in this dynamic world.
  • Prepare students for professional life for working as a business manager and entrepreneur by being fully aware of work ethics and morals.
  • Help and motivate students to pursue their further studies in this field.

Eligibility

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Students who want to enroll in BBM Program must pass the CMAT examination.
  • He/ She should have passed +2 or any equivalent level of education from any board/ institution/ University recognized by TU.
  • He/ she should have secured at least second division in proficiency level or intermediate level.

Scope and Job Prospects

Bachelor of Business Management, like other management course, has very promising scopes. The BBM graduates are enrolled in almost all sectors. One can be saleable in the most of the work place after the completion of Bachelor of Business Management degree. BBM graduates from Tribhuvan University can work in different positions, which are as follows.

  • Business Consultants
  • Business Management professional
  • Business Researcher
  • Finance Managers
  • Human Resource Manager
  • Information Systems Managers
  • Management Accountants
  • Marketing Managers
  • Production Managers
  • Research and Development Managers
